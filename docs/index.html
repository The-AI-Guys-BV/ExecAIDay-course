How it works

Intake. Install. Aftercare.

Three phases. The first is yours; we run the rest with you in the room.

1
Pre-workshop intake
You spend 30 minutes filling in a structured profile — role, industry, stack, existing systems, the things on your plate. Your install team uses this to pre-tune the vault, pre-fill connector recommendations, and draft a brief template before day one.
2
Two-day install
In person. Your install team configures the technical layers; you provide the content only you can provide — the brief content, the priorities, the real work you bring with you. By end of day two, your cockpit is running, your routines are scheduled, your meeting capture is wired.

90-day support + ongoing

The Executive Cockpit.

The named artifact you walk out with. One screen you open every morning. It refreshes against your live data on every open — calendar, urgent emails, drafts waiting, Slack mentions, yesterday's reflection, this week's GTD priorities, and one flag worth knowing about today.
